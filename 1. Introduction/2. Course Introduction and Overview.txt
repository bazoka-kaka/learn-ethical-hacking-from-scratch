COURSE INTRODUCTION AND OVERVIEW
================================

0. setting up pentesting LAB

1. Network Hacking
- how network work
- how devices communicate with each other 
- how to exploit this method of communication
to control all the connections around you, crack wifi keys, 
gain access to wifi network 
whether they use WEP, WPA, or wpa2, 
intercept data, passwords, chat messages, and anything
that anybody does on the same network

2. Gaining Access
- how to gain access to servers and pc
- learn how to gather comprehensive information 
about your target, discover vulnerabilities, and exploit them 
- create undetectable backdoors and use social engineering 
to deliver them to your target as a fake update, pdf, 
image, or any other files 
- spoof email so that they think they're 
sent from someone they know 

3. Post Exploitation
- what can you do after you get access to them 
- how to interact with the system, execute system commands, 
record keystrokes, open the webcam, use the hacked computer as a pivot
to hack another computer 

4. Website Hacking 
- how websites work, how to gather information 
about a target website, discover and exploit dangerous vulnerabilities
to gain full control over the target website 

-- at the end of each chapters, you're gonna learn how to 
protect yourself from the attacks
