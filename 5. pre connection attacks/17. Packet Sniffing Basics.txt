PACKET SNIFFING BASICS 
======================

USING AIRODUMP-NG:
- part of the aircrack-ng suit
- airodump-ng is a packet sniffer
- used to capture all packets within range 
- displays detailed info about networks around us
- connected clients...etc

use:
airodump-ng [MonitorModeInterface]

note:
this is gonna work even if we're not connected to the network, 
without knowing the key password

you have to enable monitor mode first!

- sniffing it:
> airodump-ng wlan0 

- to quit airodump-ng: 
> ctrl + c

- the things to analyze:
a. ESSID 
the names of the wireless networks around us 

b. BSSID
mac address of the target networks 

c. PWR 
signal strength of the target networks (higher better)

d. beacons
frames sent by the network to broadcast its existence

e. #/s
datas collected in the past 10 seconds
 
f. CH
the channel where the network works on 

g. MB 
maximum speed supported by the network 

h. ENC 
the encryption used by the network 

i. CIPHER 
cipher used 

j. AUTH
authentication used
